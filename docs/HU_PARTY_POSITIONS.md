## Rationale

### Fidesz-KDNP
- **EU (2):** Stays in EU for funds but actively obstructs; Orbán declared EU will "fall apart on its own"
- **Migráció (1):** Border fence, opposes all quotas, migration pact, EU relocation
- **Gazdaság (3):** National capital favouritism, cronyism; some social spending for loyalty
- **Jogállamiság (1):** Bottom of EU rule-of-law index (World Justice Project 2025); media capture
- **Ukrajna (1):** Closest EU leader to Putin; blocks sanctions, aid; "Ukraine is our enemy"
- **Környezet (2):** Opposes EU climate agenda; mocks von der Leyen's climate policies

### TISZA (Péter Magyar)
- **EU (4):** Pro-EU, Western orientation; wants Hungary back in mainstream EU
- **Migráció (2):** Opposes mandatory quotas and EU migration pact — closer to Fidesz on this issue
- **Gazdaság (3):** Centrist; economic reform promised but details vague
- **Jogállamiság (4):** Core campaign promise — restore judicial independence, press freedom
- **Ukrajna (3):** Pro-peace but opposes accelerated Ukraine EU accession; fight Russian propaganda
- **Környezet (3):** Moderate — supports EU green agenda without strong domestic commitments

### Mi Hazánk (László Toroczkai)
- **EU (1):** Explicitly anti-EU integration (Chapel Hill data); ESN affiliation
- **Migráció (1):** Far-right, hardline anti-migration
- **Gazdaság (2):** Nationalist economic policy; anti-globalist
- **Jogállamiság (2):** Nationalist populist; some anti-corruption rhetoric but authoritarian tendencies
- **Ukrajna (1):** Pro-Russia, anti-Ukraine; opposes any aid or accession
- **Környezet (2):** Nationalist framing; skeptical of EU green agenda

### DK (Demokratikus Koalíció — Ferenc Gyurcsány)
- **EU (5):** Strongly pro-EU, federalist tendencies
- **Migráció (3):** More open than TISZA; accepts EU framework
- **Gazdaság (4):** Social democratic; higher wages, public investment
- **Jogállamiság (5):** Strongest rule-of-law platform among mainstream parties
- **Ukrajna (5):** Strongly pro-Ukraine, supports aid and EU accession
- **Környezet (4):** Green agenda supporter; EU climate alignment

### MKKP (Magyar Kétfarkú Kutya Párt — satirical/protest)
- **EU (4):** Pro-EU; satirises Orbán's Euroscepticism
- **Migráció (3):** Moderate; mocks anti-migration hysteria
- **Gazdaság (4):** Progressive; satirical but genuine policy proposals (UBI experiments)
- **Jogállamiság (5):** Anti-corruption core identity; transparency maximalists
- **Ukrajna (4):** Pro-Ukraine solidarity
- **Környezet (5):** Strongest environmental platform; ran trees-for-votes campaigns, literal tree planting
